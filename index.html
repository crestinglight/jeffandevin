Details

Everything you need to know about the big day.

When & Where

Our big day is on October 20, 2018. Both the ceremony and reception will take place at Castle Unicorn in Glenwood, IA, about a half hour east of Omaha.

Ceremony

Guests may arrive at 4:45 PM, and the ceremony will begin at 5:15 PM. Feel free to take plenty of pictures, but please make sure your flash is off.

Reception

After the ceremony, we hope you will all enjoy a cocktail hour, then make your way over to the reception area for dinner.

Food

Dinner will be served buffet style with -option- -option-. Please let us know if you have any special dietary requirements such as gluten sensitivity or vegetarian options, we will make sure you are taken care of!

Drinks

After the ceremony, we will have a 3-hour block of open bar. During this time, beer and wine is on us! After that time, the full bar will remain open as a cash bar. Iced tea and lemonade will be available all night as well.

Attire

This is not a black tie event, semi-formal attire will be perfect. If you would like to dress with our color scheme, we will be using autumn reds, oranges, and plum, but please wear whatever you are comfortable with!

Travel

We are so grateful that many of our guests are making the trek from out of town. Click here for directions to the hotel, and here for directions to the venue.

Weather

Weather around this time of year can be a little unpredictable, we expect temperatures between 55 and 70 degrees. Rain or snow will not be likely, but be sure to bring a light jacket.
